Dataset Description

This project uses the publicly available RoadTracer dataset provided by the authors of the following paper:

X. Wang, L. Wang, W. Huang, Y. Liu, S. Zhang, and X. Zhong,
"Local–Global Sparse Transformer for Road Extraction From Remote Sensing Imagery,"
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, vol. 63, pp. 1–10, 2025,
Art no. 5642410, doi: 10.1109/TGRS.2025.3598813.

Dataset Download
