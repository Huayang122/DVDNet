Dataset Description

Dataset Download
The dataset is officially released as part of the DeepGlobe Challenge and can be accessed via:
https://competitions.codalab.org/competitions/18467
Alternatively, a mirrored version is available on Kaggle:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/balraj98/deepglobe-road-extraction-dataset

Dataset Details
The dataset comprises 8,570 high-resolution remote sensing images. Among them:
- 6,226 images are provided with annotations
- 1,243 validation images and 1,101 test images are without annotations

For our experiments, the 6,226 annotated images were re-partitioned for experimental purposes.
